About Us

Welcome to 057 Cafe, your neighborhood Grill home right on Stateway in Welkom. Our name comes from the 057 phone code because we are proud of our town and our people.We opened our doors with a simple goal. We wanted to build a place where local people could connect, relax, enjoy great food and the best music. At 057 Cafe, we care about more than just business. We care about human connection. Our space features comfortable seating, bright lighting, and a friendly team that treats you like family. Whether you are catching up with an old friend, working on your laptop, or grabbing a quick bite before work, you belong here.
